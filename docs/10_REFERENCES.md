# References
Laser Cooling — Venus Cooling Project

## Purpose

This document lists **scientific, technical, and educational references** relevant
to the Laser Cooling project. Sources focus on radiative physics, planetary energy
balance, laser fundamentals, and Venus-specific data.

The list prioritizes widely accepted, foundational material.
